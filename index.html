What is a Backyard Ultra?

The Bristol Backyard Ultra is a unique endurance event where runners complete a 4.167-mile loop every hour, on the hour — until only one runner remains. It’s not about speed — it’s about outlasting the competition. Hosted on private trails just outside Bristol, this event is about strategy, grit, and mental strength.
